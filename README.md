## 🎯 Motivation and Objectives

The **Chestnut Ridge Supersite (CHESS)** is a new platform for collaborative research into biosphere-atmosphere interactions. Key objectives include:

- **Introducing CHESS** as a new scientific supersite.
- Advancing our understanding of **soil-forest-atmosphere exchanges**.
- Fostering collaboration among atmospheric scientists, ecologists, and other researchers.
- Encouraging the deployment of complementary scientific instruments for enhanced data collection.

CHESS provides open access to high-quality atmospheric and ecological data, supporting both new and ongoing research in the region.
